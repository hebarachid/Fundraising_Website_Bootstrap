‘I Loved Going to School, but We Don’t Have Enough Money’

Lina, 12, is from Kapisa Province in northeastern Afghanistan, but her family was displaced by fighting seven years ago. She now lives in a refugee camp in Kabul. She went to school for three years before being pulled out. I loved going to school, but we don’t have enough money to buy notebooks and other things. Our relatives are angry at us for leaving school, but without notebooks it was not possible to study and do homework.
