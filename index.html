How we started

End Poverty

2017

"Humanity is calling" is founded. My parents envisioned an unbiased and transparent source of information that would assist givers from every state, and with every type of charitable interest, in finding a charity to support.

2018

To help donors avoid becoming victims of mailing-list appeals, "Humanity is calling " begins to assess each charity's commitment to keeping donors' personal information confidential. It helped almost 2700 children in its first active years by providing food, shelter and cloth to the unfortunates

2019

"Humanity is calling " launches its enhanced Financial Health rating methodology. This update captures an organization’s long-term capacity and health by allowing for higher scores in program expense. And it called for volunteer recruitment, gaining 134! new volunteers

2020

The pandemic is decreasing our civil-movement, however children and unfortunates needs us now more than ever . We are trying to provide medication for the old people, and enough support for children to remain safe at home if they have one. Donate please ,we need you!
